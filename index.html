About me

Hello! My name is Dagmar.

Welcome to my internet webpage. It's not much, but enjoy the text as well as the images.

I'm a graduate student and member of the GRAIL lab at the University of Alberta. I have a BSc in research based computer science from Reykjavík University.

Research interests

My primary research interests exist in the intersection of AI and art. I'm currently working on procedural content generation via machine learning to create co-creative tools for artists.

I am also broadly interested in machine learning and I'm always open to having a chat with fellow students and practitioners.
